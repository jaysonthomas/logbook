Skills

Control

  • Developing and tuning low-level control systems for actuators.
    • Current/Torque
    • Velocity
    • Position
    • Impedance
    • Admittance
  • MPC

Modeling & simulation

  • Multi body forward/inverse kinematics and dynamics models.
    • Use these models to predict actuator behavior ensuring accurate motion planning and control.
    • Address gravity and inertia compensation.

  • Use physics engines (e.g., MuJoCo, Drake) to simulate the entire robotic body using URDF. Simulate multiple actuators and environmental interactions, to test and refine system performance.

Actuators

  • Test and characterisation.

  • Create models for actuators under different conditions (e.g., load, speed).
  • Actuation sizing (torque, speed) based on performance criteria for robotic applications such as manipulation and bipedal locomotion.

  • Validation of actuators in robotic systems.
  • Analysis (and compensation) of dynamic parameters such as friction, backdrivability, backlash and hysteresis.
