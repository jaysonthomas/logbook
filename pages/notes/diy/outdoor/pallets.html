Taking pallets apart

This is a good video on how to take pallets apart. Just need a pry bar, a sledge hammer and another wood-piece to concentrate the force between the hammer and the pallet. Also, love the guy's pallet-table.

Pay attention to the nails. Also, it will be interesting to see which wood pieces will split easily.
