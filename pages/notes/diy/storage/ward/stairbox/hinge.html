Sizing up a tee-hinge

Door measurements

Based on wickes website, the floorboards are made of softwood. From here, the density of pine is $500 \frac{kg}{m^3}$.

The volume of 6.0625 floorboards $$\begin{align*} v &= 2532 \times 144 \times 18 \times 6.0625 \\ &= 39787848 \, mm^3 \\ &= 0.039787 \, m^3 \end{align*}$$

Total weight $$\begin{align*} m &= \rho \times v \\ &= 500 \times 0.039787 \\ &= 19.89 \, kg \end{align*}$$
