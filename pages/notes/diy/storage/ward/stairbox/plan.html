Step 1

First shell

Step 1

Check for level, rail-fit and consider where hinges are going to go when screwing.
  • Build frame out of 4 studs for 1st set of doors.
  • Make holes for hanging rail in 2 horizontal studs.
  • Screw 3 horizontal studs into left most wall.
  • Screw left most door stud to wall, stair box and horizontal studs.
  • Screw vertical stud for middle partition to wall.
  • Screw middle stud to stairbox.
  • Screw 3 horizontal studs between the 2 middle studs.
