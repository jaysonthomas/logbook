Steps

Attach Stud to wall

  • Have a rough idea of what material is in the wall by tapping.
  • Choose/buy right drill bits to make a hole in the wall.
    • First create a hole using a screw
    • Use the 6 mm masonry drill bit
    • Might have to use pin if it's hollow behind the plasterboard

  • Choose screw and wall plug.
    • Wall plug: 30 (l), 6 (diameter)
    • Screw: Gold, 5 (diameter), 70 (l)
    • Drill bit: Masonry, 6 (diameter)
