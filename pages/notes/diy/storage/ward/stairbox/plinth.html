Plinth

Aim

Plinth is the lowest horizontal structural member of a construction. It helps:
  • Spread the weight of the wardrobes evenly across the floor
  • Adapt to any unevenness in the floor

Dimensions

  • Height or Length
  • Width: 100 mm
  • Depth or thickness: 18 mm
The timber used for the attic is called Studwork CLS Timber of the following dimensions:
  • Length: 2400 mm
  • Width: 63 mm
  • Depth: 38 mm
It also comes with a width of 89 mm. In the comments, people use this to create stud walls. But, even the 63 mm is advertised to be useful for creating stud walls.
