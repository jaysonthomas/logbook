Loft lighting

Wiring 1 trial light

  • 1 Gang light back box
  • Cut into the live wires coming into/going out of a light point; not the switch wire

  • Use volt pen to ensure no power is present in the wire
    • Voltage detector pen and socket tester: here.

  • Wire stripper for T&E cables
  • Earth sleeving

  • 221 wago connectors
    • 5 way connector pack, 3 way connector

  • 1.5mm T&E wire reel here

  • Wago boxes
    • Junction box, Lighting junction box, Bigger junction box.

  • Lights
    • Trying out 3000k temperature light bulb with GU10 base and 500lm brightness. A Light temperature guide. This 570lm, 2700K, 6W bulb is what I use for the Ikea lamps.

  • Light bulb holders
    • Wowatt ceiling bar

  • Light switch
    • Front plate, Back plate, Back pattress box screws, Electrical screws for front plate

  • Screws
    • 6 by 120 mm length screws.

  • Super glue

Steps

[Ref]
  • Wire up 1 light
  • Look for the wire coming out of one of the lights
    • It should be a 1.5mm twin and earth wiring.
  • Split the wire and attach them using a 3 terminal wago connector
  • Place light switch next to the hatch
  • Attach switch to the spliced wire junction
  • Attach a 2.5mm wiring for the loft light to the spliced wire junction
  • Tidy up wire to the loft light using cable clips
  • Get Wire strippers, light wago box, and just a normal wago box, 1.5mm/2.5mm twin and earth wire, lights, switch box, earth sheath, switch wire sheath.
