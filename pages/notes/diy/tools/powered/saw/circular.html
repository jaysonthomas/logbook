Tutorials

  • Safety saw for beginners part 1, part 2
    • Safety glasses, speed square, adjust teeth depth 4-6 mm (or just 1T) beyond the lower surface of the object, place hand on the surface or on the saw itself or on the provided guard. Always stand to the side of the saw.
  • How to cut straight with no guide: ref

  • Kickback
    • To avoid kickbacks, support the wood properly and don't change the direction of cut while sawing else the wood pieces on either side press against the sides of the blade preventing it from rolling freely.
