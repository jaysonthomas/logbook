Wood screws

Overview

The following sections are organised based on the screw diameter. In screw specifications, the diameter always comes first. The head diameter is nearly twice the diameter of the screw. The length is from the screw head. Assume fully threaded with single threads, unless specified otherwise. If a screw is partially threaded, it would be near the end.
