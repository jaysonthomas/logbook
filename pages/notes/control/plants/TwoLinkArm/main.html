Two-link arm

Topics

A 2-link arm can also be considered to be a 2R robot or a top-down double pendulum.

    • IK can yield multiple joint solutions leading to discontinuity. Diff IK is used instead but a Jacobian is not always a square matrix; hence taking the inverse becomes a problem. Pseudo-inverse Jacobian can be rectangular; it tries to solve an optimisation problem to find the minimum joint angles that can achieve the desired ee spatial velocity, as close as possible.
