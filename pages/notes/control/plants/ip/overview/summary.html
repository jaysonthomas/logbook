Cart-Pole: Summary

Pole kinematics

The projection of the pole on the X-Y axes is dependent on its angle. Taking a double derivative gives its acceleration w.r.t. A. To get the pole's acceleration w.r.t. W, its linear acceleration component due to the motion of the cart is summed together. Does the contribution (towards the overall pole's acceleration) of the acceleration component due to the cart's motion get subtracted from by the pole's projected acceleration due to its rotational motion?
