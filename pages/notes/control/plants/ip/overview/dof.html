Cartpole kinematics: Degrees of freedom

Overview

Possibilities

Since the translation and rotation of the system is constrained in the xy plane and about the $\hat{k}$ axis respectively, 6 DoFs are possible. Each object (cart and pole) can have 2 translatory motions in the $\hat{i}, \hat{j}$ directions and 1 rotation.

Constraints

The cart can only move in the $\hat{i}$ direction, determined by the state variable $x$.

Since the pole is fixed at A:

  • All points points on it have the same translation motion as that of the cart
  • It is constrained to only rotate about $\hat{k}$

Thus there are 2 DOFs, requiring 2 coordinates [$x, \theta$] to be defined (hence, 2 FBD) and yielding 2 equations of motion.
