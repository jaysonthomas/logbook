Cart-Pole: Aim

Overview

The goal is to:
  • Understand how the angle of an inverted pendulum (pole) changes when a force is applied to the cart it is mounted to.

  • Derive the equations of motion that relates the acceleration/velocity/position of the cart to the angle/angular velocity of the pole.

    • This involves finding $\ddot{x}, \ddot{\theta}$, which can then be integrated to find the other parameters given some initial condition. This is how simulations work.

  • Derive a mathematical description of the dynamics of the system, i.e. of how the system moves.

Features

  • It is unstable without control, i.e., the pendulum will fall over if the cart isn't moved to balance it.
  • The dynamics of the system are nonlinear.

Real-world example

Attitude control of a booster rocket at takeoff.
