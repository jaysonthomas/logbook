Cart-pole dynamics: Alternate equations of acceleration

An alternate equation for $\ddot{\theta}$

Combining the $\ddot{x}$ equations from the cart and pole: $$\begin{align*} \frac{f + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l(\ddot\theta \, sin\theta + \dot\theta^2 \, cos\theta)} {m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}} &= \frac{-J \ddot\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2} {m^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta} \\ fm^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta + (m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2cos\theta(\ddot\theta \, sin\theta + \dot\theta^2 \, cos\theta) &= -J ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}})\ddot\theta + ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}}) (m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2) \\ (m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2\ddot\theta cos\theta\, sin\theta + J ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}})\ddot\theta &= ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}}) (m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2) - fm^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta - (m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2\dot\theta^2cos^2\theta \\\\ \end{align*}$$ $$\begin{align*} \ddot\theta = \frac{({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}}) (m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2) - fm^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta - (m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2\dot\theta^2cos^2\theta} {(m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2 cos\theta\, sin\theta + J ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}})} \tag{5}\\ \end{align*}$$

An alternate equation for $\ddot{x}$

Combining the $\ddot{\theta}$ equations from the cart and pole: $$\begin{align*} &\frac{(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) \ddot{x} - m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f} {m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \sin\theta} = \frac{m^{\scriptsize{p}}l \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 - \ddot{x} \,cos\theta + g sin\theta\bigr]}{J} \\ &J((m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) \ddot{x} - m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f) = m^{\scriptsize{p}}l \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 - \ddot{x} \,cos\theta + g sin\theta\bigr] (m^{\scriptsize{p}} l sin\theta) \\ &J(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) \ddot{x} - J(m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f) = -{m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \,cos\theta \ddot{x} + {m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 + g sin\theta\bigr] \\ &J(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) \ddot{x} + {m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \,cos\theta \ddot{x} = {m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 + g sin\theta\bigr] + J(m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f) \\ \end{align*}$$ $$\begin{align*} \ddot{x} = \frac{{m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 + g sin\theta\bigr] + J(m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f)} {J(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) + {m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \,cos\theta} \tag{6} \end{align*}$$
