Cart Pole

Cartpole: Balance of forces about the centre of mass

Cart

A is the point at which the mass of the cart is concentrated. $$\begin{align} \newcommand{\WaG}{^{\scriptsize{W}\hspace{-0.8em}}a^{\scriptsize{G}}} \sum F_\hat{i}^{\scriptsize{A}} &= m^{\scriptsize{A}} \ddot{x} \,\hat{i} \nonumber \\ &= (-kx -b\dot{x} + F_2\, cos\theta + F_1\, sin\theta) \,\hat{i} \label{eq:iHatCartForces} \end{align}$$

Pole

$$\begin{align} \sum F_\hat{d}^{\scriptsize{G}} &= m^{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG \,\hat{d} \nonumber \\ &= -F_1 \,\hat{d} + m^{\scriptsize{G}}g\,cos\theta \,\hat{d} \label{eq:dHatPoleForces} \\ \sum F_\hat{q}^{\scriptsize{G}} &= m^{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG \,\hat{q} \nonumber \\ &= -F_2 \,\hat{q} - m^{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta \,\hat{q} \label{eq:qHatPoleForces} \end{align}$$ $^{\scriptsize{W}\hspace{-0.4em}}a^{\scriptsize{G}}\,\hat{d}$ is the acceleration of the centre of gravity/mass of the pole w.r.t. the world frame in the $\hat{d}$ direction.
