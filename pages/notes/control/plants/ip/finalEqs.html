Final equations

Overview

Substituting $F_1$ and $F_2$ in the equation for balance of forces on the cart: $$\begin{align*} m_{\scriptsize{A}} \ddot{x} &= -kx -b\dot{x} + F_2\, cos\theta + F_1\, sin\theta + f_x \\ &= -kx -b\dot{x} + \bigl(-m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} (\ddot{x}\,cos\theta + l\ddot\theta)\bigr) \, cos\theta + \bigl(m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,cos\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} (\ddot{x}\,sin\theta - l\dot\theta^2)\bigr)\, sin\theta + f_x \\ &= -kx -b\dot{x} - m_{\scriptsize{G}} \ddot{x} - m_{\scriptsize{G}} l\ddot\theta \, cos\theta + m_{\scriptsize{G}} l\dot\theta^2 \, sin\theta + f_x \\ \ddot{x} &= \frac{-kx -b\dot{x} - m_{\scriptsize{G}} l\ddot\theta \, cos\theta + m_{\scriptsize{G}} l\dot\theta^2 \, sin\theta + f_x} {m_{\scriptsize{A}} + m_{\scriptsize{G}}} \end{align*}$$ Substituting $F_2$ in the equation for balance of moments on the pole: $$\begin{align*} \ddot\theta &= \frac{lF_2}{J} \\ &= \frac{\bigl(-m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} (\ddot{x}\,cos\theta + l\ddot\theta) \bigr)l}{J} \\ J \ddot\theta &= -lm_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - lm_{\scriptsize{G}} \ddot{x}\,cos\theta - l^2m_{\scriptsize{G}}\ddot\theta \\ \ddot\theta &= \frac{-lm_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - lm_{\scriptsize{G}} \ddot{x}\,cos\theta} {J + l^2m_{\scriptsize{G}}} \end{align*}$$
