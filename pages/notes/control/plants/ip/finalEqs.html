Cart-pole dynamics: Alternate equations of acceleration

Equations due to forces on the pole

$$\begin{align*} \ddot{x} &= \frac{-J \ddot\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2} {m^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta} \tag{1} \label{eq:1} \\\\ \ddot\theta &= \frac{m^{\scriptsize{p}}l \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 - \ddot{x} \,cos\theta + g sin\theta\bigr]}{J} \tag{2} \label{eq:2} \end{align*}$$

Equations due to forces on the cart

$$\begin{align*} \ddot{x} &= \frac{f + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l(\ddot\theta \, sin\theta + \dot\theta^2 \, cos\theta)} {m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}} \tag{3} \label{eq:3} \\\\ \ddot{\theta} &= \frac{(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) \ddot{x} - m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f} {m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \sin\theta} \tag{4} \label{eq:4} \end{align*}$$

Derived equations from above

$$\begin{align*} \ddot\theta &= \frac{({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}}) (m^{\scriptsize{p}}g l\,sin\theta + m^{\scriptsize{p}}l^2 \dot\theta^2) - fm^{\scriptsize{p}}l\,cos\theta - (m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2\dot\theta^2cos^2\theta} {(m^{\scriptsize{p}})^2l^2 cos\theta\, sin\theta + J ({m^{\scriptsize{p}} + m^{\scriptsize{c}}})} \tag{5} \label{eq:5}\\\\ \ddot{x} &= \frac{{m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \bigl[l \dot\theta^2 + g sin\theta\bigr] + J(m^{\scriptsize{p}} l \dot{\theta}^2 \cos\theta - f)} {J(m^{\scriptsize{c}} + m^{\scriptsize{p}}) + {m^{\scriptsize{p}}}^2l^2 sin\theta \,cos\theta} \tag{6} \label{eq:6} \end{align*}$$

Usage

Equations that can be used together:
  • \ref{eq:5} and \ref{eq:3}
  • \ref{eq:6} and \ref{eq:2}
  • \ref{eq:2} and \ref{eq:3}
