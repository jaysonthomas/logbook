Pole dynamics: Balance of forces

Overview

Considering linear acceleration of G w.r.t. W in the different directions in the body frame: $$\begin{align*} \newcommand{\wrt}[5] { ^{\scriptsize{#1}\hspace{#2em}} {#3} ^{\scriptsize{#4}} _{\scriptsize\hat{#5}} } \newcommand{\WaG}[1]{\wrt{W}{-0.5}{a}{G}{#1}} \newcommand{eqRef}[1]{\tag{#1} \label{eq:#1}} \sum F_\hat{d}^{\scriptsize{G}} &= m_{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG{d} \\ &= -F_1 + m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,cos\theta \\ F_1 &= m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,cos\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG{d} \\ &= m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,cos\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} (\ddot{x}\,sin\theta - l\dot\theta^2)\eqRef{1} \\\\ \sum F_\hat{q}^{\scriptsize{G}} &= m_{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG{q} \\ &= -F_2 - m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta \\ F_2 &= -m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} \,\WaG{q} \\ &= -m_{\scriptsize{G}}g\,sin\theta - m_{\scriptsize{G}} (\ddot{x}\,cos\theta + l\ddot\theta) \eqRef{2} \end{align*}$$ $^{\scriptsize{W}\hspace{-0.4em}}a^{\scriptsize{G}}\,\hat{d}$ is the acceleration of the centre of gravity/mass of the pole w.r.t. the world frame in the $\hat{d}$ direction.
