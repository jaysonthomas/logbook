Pole dynamics: Moment due to gravity

Overview

The torque at A resulting from the downward pull of gravity on the pendulum's mass at G is given by: $$ (M^{\scriptsize{A}}_g)\,\hat{k} = -m_{\scriptsize{p}} g \,sin\theta \cdot l $$ It is zero when the pendulum is fully vertical, and negative when the pendulum is at $+90^0$, i.e. accelerating the pendulum with a negative torque rotates it in the clockwise direction.
