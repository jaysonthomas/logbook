Dynamics

Forces/moments

The following forces acting on a pendulum result in torques about its pivot point:
  • Force due to gravity acting on the pendulum's point mass at G
  • Inertial moment
  • Tension on the string balancing the gravity component along the string

  • Damping forces such as friction at the pivot point and air resistance
    • $$ \newcommand{\wrt}[4]{#1^{\hspace{#2em}\scriptsize{#3}}_{\hspace{#2em}#4}} (\wrt{M}{-0.2}{A}{f})\,\hat{k} = -b\,\dot\theta(t) $$ $b$ represents the damping coefficient which characterizes the amount of damping present in the system.

  • Any externally applied forces resulting in torque $u(t)$

Equation of motion

The torques acting on a pendulum must be in equilibrium as per d'Alembert's principle, giving an equation of motion for the pendulum model. $$\begin{align*} (\wrt{M}{-0.2}{A}{f} + \wrt{M}{-0.1}{A}{g} + \wrt{M}{-0.1}{A}{i} + u)\,\hat{k} &= 0 \\ (-b\,\dot\theta) + (-m_{\scriptsize{p}} g \,sin\theta \cdot l) - (m_pl^2 \ddot\theta) + u &= 0 \end{align*}$$ $$\begin{align} \ddot\theta = \frac{u - b\,\dot\theta -m_{\scriptsize{p}} g \,sin\theta \cdot l}{m_pl^2} \label{eq:1} \end{align}$$

State space form

Dynamics of a pendulum in a first-order form: $$\begin{align*} \dot{x} &= f(x,u) \\ \frac{d}{dt}\begin{bmatrix} \theta \\ \dot{\theta} \end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix} \dot{\theta} \\ \frac{u - b\dot\theta -m_p g \,sin\theta \cdot l}{m_pl^2} \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}$$ where $x=\begin{bmatrix}\theta \\ \dot{\theta}\end{bmatrix}$ is the state vector, and $u$ is the input torque which is the only control knob available.

In code, the dynamics function takes in $x$, $u$ and some parameters to return back $\dot{x}$. $\dot{x}$ decides the next state which is then, along with $u$, used to calculate the next value of $\dot{x}$.

$q$ or $\theta$ is not a vector that lives on the real line, i.e. is not $\mathbb{R}^n$. It is a compact manifold that wraps around (it's a circle) and does not go to infinity. $$\begin{align*} q &\in S^1 \quad &\text{(circle)} \\ v &\in \mathbb{R} &\\ x &\in S^1 \times \mathbb{R} \quad &\text{(cylinder)} \end{align*}$$

Control affine form

$$\begin{align*} \dot{x} &= f_0(x) + B(x)\, u \\ \begin{bmatrix} \dot\theta \\ \ddot{\theta} \end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix} \dot\theta \\ \frac{-g sin\theta}{l} \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ \frac{1}{m_p l^2} \end{bmatrix} \, u \end{align*}$$

Manipulator form

$$\begin{align*} M(q) &= m_p l^2 \\ C(q, v) &= m_p gl sin\theta \\ B &= I \\ G &= I \end{align*}$$
