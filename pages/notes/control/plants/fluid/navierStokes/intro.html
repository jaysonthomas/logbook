Overview

Navier Stokes equation consists of 2 main equations:
  • Momentum equation
  • Mass conservation or continuity equation
For an incompressible fluid like water, the equations describe a balance of forces. At a high level, these equations are a fluid-based version of Newton's Second Law of Motion: $$ F = ma $$ The descriptions in the following sections are based on considering a cube of water moving through a pipe.
