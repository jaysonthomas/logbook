Recursive definition

Consider the linearised discrete time dynamics with the origin shifted to the equilibrium point: $$ x_{k+1} \approx A_d \, x_k $$ Consider the current state is $x_1$. Then the next state is given as: $$\begin{align*} x_2 &= A_d \, x_1 \\ &= A_d (A_d \, x_0) \\ &= A_d^2 \, x_0 \end{align*}$$ This can be generalised as: $$ x_{k+1} = A_d^k \, x_0 $$
