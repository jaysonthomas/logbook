Definitions

Dynamics

It is the branch of mechanics that is concerned with the study of forces and their effects on motion.

The dynamics problem

Given the forces/moments acting on a system, can a future state of a system be predicted.

The control problem

Given a desired motion and the current state of a system, what forces and moments need to be commanded to achieve the desired motion.

Smooth vs Non-smooth systems

Robotics has contact mechanics when a foot hits the ground or a gripper grasps an object. Making and breaking contact is non-smooth, resulting in a discontinuous impact that cannot be written as an ODE.

$F=ma$ does not apply at the instant when contact is made or broken.

State vector

It is everything that is needed to specify the initial conditions of a system.

Affine

It means linear plus a constant: $ax + b$

Euler-Lagrange equation

Kinetic energy minus potential energy. $$ L = \frac{1}{2} v^T M(q) v - U(q) $$
