Advance State

Overview

$$ X_{n+1} = X + (\dot{X} \cdot dt) $$ $$\begin{align*} X = \begin{bmatrix} x & y & z & \phi & \theta & \psi& \dot{x} & \dot{y} & \dot{z} & p & q & r \end{bmatrix}^T \end{align*}$$ $$\begin{align*} X = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{x} & \dot{y} & \dot{z} & \dot\phi & \dot\theta & \dot\psi& \ddot{x} & \ddot{y} & \ddot{z} & \dot{p} & \dot{q} & \dot{r} \end{bmatrix}^T \end{align*}$$ $\dot{x},\dot{y},\dot{z}$ - This is already present in the state.

$\dot\phi, \dot\theta, \dot\psi$ - Angular velocities in world frame

$\ddot{x}, \ddot{y}, \ddot{z}$ - Linear accelerations

$\dot{p}, \dot{q}, \dot{r}$ - Angular accelerations in body frame
