Properties of drone class

Angles and angular rates

  • Euler angles [$rad$] in world frame: $\phi, \theta, \psi$
  • Body rates [$\frac{rad}{s}$] in body frame: $p,q,r$

Forces

  • Forces generated by the propellers
    • $$\begin{align*} F_1 &= k_f \omega_1^2 \\ F_2 &= k_f \omega_2^2 \\ F_3 &= k_f \omega_3^2 \\ F_4 &= k_f \omega_4^2 \\ \end{align*}$$
  • Collective upward force
    • $$ F_{total} = F_1 + F_2 + F_3 + F_4 $$

Moments

  • Reactive moments
    • $$\begin{align*} \tau_1 &= -k_m \omega_1^2 \\ \tau_2 &= k_m \omega_2^2 \\ \tau_3 &= -k_m \omega_3^2 \\ \tau_4 &= k_m \omega_4^2 \\ \end{align*}$$
  • Moment about each axis
    • $$\begin{align*} \tau_x &= \Big(|F_1| + |F_4| - |F_2| - |F_3|\Big)l \\ \tau_y &= \Big(|F_1| + |F_2| - |F_3| - |F_4|\Big)l \\ \tau_z &= \tau_1 + \tau_2 + \tau_3 + \tau_4 \end{align*}$$
