2D Drone dynamics: Overview

Steps

  • Define the state
    • The state describes the minimal amount of information needed to predict the motion from one time step to the next. For 2D, the state variables are the vehicle's lateral y-position, vertical z-position, and roll angle, $\phi$. The state also includes velocities associated with each of these variables, which gives a total of six state variables.

  • Figure out forces and moments acting on the vehicle.

  • Convert forces and moments into linear and angular accelerations by applying $F = m*a$, and its rotational equivalent.

  • Integrate accelerations to get the velocities which is then integrated again to get positions.
advance_state(..) captures these dynamics.
