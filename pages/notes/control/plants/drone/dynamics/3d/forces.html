3D Drone dynamics: Forces

Overview

Forces on a drone come from the rotation of the motors. In a body fixed frame, the location of the motors are fixed which makes the math simple - all four motor forces point up in the frame of the body. The net force in the body frame will be the collective thrust from the four rotors and it will be negative in the z direction, since down is positive and up is negative.

The net torque becomes simple to calculate in the body frame as well, since the drone's orientation doesn't matter.
