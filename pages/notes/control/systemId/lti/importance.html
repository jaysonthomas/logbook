LTI: Importance of linear system representation

The criteria for a system to be classified as LTI is so severe that no real physical system can meet them. There is always some aspect of non-linearity or variation over time in the real world.

Reasons for modeling systems as LTI:

  • There are lots of mathematical tools that are capable of solving them.
    • Alternatively, only very simple non-LTI systems are solvable currently.

  • There are a wide range of physical systems that can be modleled or approximated very accurately with an LTI model.
    • As long as a system behaves linearly over some region of operation, then it can be treated as LTI over the region.
