Motivation

Overview

  • Solve multiple robot tasks that are in conflict
    • Consider a humanoid with a camera mounted. The aim is to continuously see a ball and also try to reach out for it and grasp it. These tasks conflict with each other under the assumption that the humanoid needs to stay in balance.

  • At a fast rate
    • So that the computation can be incorporated with a control framework.

  • Under constraints
    • The above points are always considered within some constraints like respecting joint limits.
