Use

It always adds artificial damping and hence causes an undershoot. By default, most simulators (Mujoco, Bullet, Isaac) use this. The artificial damping is desirable because it means the simulator won't blow up and do unphysical things, but it is still wrong.

It is very common to use implicit integrators when dealing with stiff systems or contact problems since they have better stability properties.
