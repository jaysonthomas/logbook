Discretisation

Explicit form

$$ x_{k+1} = f_d(x_k, u_k) $$ $f_d$: Discrete function.

Forward Euler integration

Math

$$ x_{k+1} = \underbrace{x_k + h \, f(x_k, u_k)}_{f_d(x_k, u_k)} $$ $h$ is the time step.
