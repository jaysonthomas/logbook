Cost function: Refined approach

The vector of optimal control inputs $u$ can be determined by minimising the following cost function: $$ J = \min_u J_z + J_u $$ From the penalty and the tracking error, the cost function can be rewritten as: $$\begin{align*} J &= (s - Mu)^T \, W_P \, (s - Mu) + u^T W u \\ &= s^T \, W_P \,s - s^T \, W_P \, Mu - u^T \, M^T \, W_P \, s + u^T \, M^T \, W_P \, Mu \, + u^T W u \end{align*}$$
