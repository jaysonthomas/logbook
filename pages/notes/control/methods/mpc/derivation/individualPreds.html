Individual predictions at the current time step

At time instant $k$, the controller predicts the state and output vectors up to $k+p$. It is assumed the state at $k$ is perfectly known. From the state/measurement update models, the following state and output predictions are made.

Prediction at $k+1$

$$\begin{align*} x_{k+1|k} &= Ax_k + Bu_{k|k} \\ z_{k+1|k} &= Cx_{k+1|k} \\ &= CAx_k + CBu_{k|k} \end{align*}$$

Prediction at $k+2$

$$\begin{align*} x_{k+2|k} &= Ax_{k+1} + Bu_{k+1|k} \\ &= A^2x_k + ABu_{k|k} + Bu_{k+1|k} \\ z_{k+2|k} &= Cx_{k+2|k} \\ &= CA^2x_k + CABu_{k|k} + CBu_{k+1|k} \end{align*}$$

Prediction at $k+3$

$$\begin{align*} x_{k+3|k} &= Ax_{k+2} + Bu_{k+2|k} \\ &= A^3x_k + A^2Bu_{k|k} + ABu_{k+1|k} + Bu_{k+2|k} \\ z_{k+3|k} &= Cx_{k+3|k} \\ &= CA^3x_k + CA^2Bu_{k|k} + CABu_{k+1|k} + CBu_{k+2|k} \\ &= CA^3x_k + \begin{bmatrix} CA^2B & CAB & CB \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}$$

Prediction at the end of the control horizon c

$$ z_{k+c|k} = CA^cx_k + \begin{bmatrix} CA^{c-1}B & CA^{c-2}B & \cdots & CAB & CB \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \\ \vdots \\ u_{k+c-2|k} \\ u_{k+c-1|k} \end{bmatrix} $$

Prediction at $k+c+1$

After index $c$, the torque remains constant. This is a constraint imposed on the controller. $$ u_{k+c|k} = u_{k+c+1|k} = \cdots = u_{k+c+p|k} $$ Therefore, at $k+c+1$: $$ z_{k+c+1|k} = CA^{c+1}x_k + \begin{bmatrix} CA^cB & CA^{c-1}B & \cdots & CA^2B & C(A +I)B \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \\ \vdots \\ u_{k+c-2|k} \\ u_{k+c-1|k} \end{bmatrix} $$

Prediction at the final time step $k+p$

$$\begin{align*} z_{k+p|k} &= CA^{p}x_k + \begin{bmatrix} CA^{p-1}B & CA^{p-2}B & \cdots & CA^{p-c+1}B & C(A^{p-c} + A^{p-c-1} + \cdots + A + I)B \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \\ \vdots \\ u_{k+c-2|k} \\ u_{k+c-1|k} \end{bmatrix} \\ &= CA^{p}x_k + \begin{bmatrix} CA^{p-1}B & CA^{p-2}B & \cdots & CA^{p-c+1}B & C\bar{A}_{p,c}B \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \\ \vdots \\ u_{k+c-2|k} \\ u_{k+c-1|k} \end{bmatrix} \\ \end{align*}$$ where $\bar{A}_{p,c} = A^{p-c} + A^{p-c-1} + \cdots + A + I$ For any general $i > v$: $$ \bar{A}_{i,c} = A^{i-c} + A^{i-c-1} + \cdots + A + I $$
