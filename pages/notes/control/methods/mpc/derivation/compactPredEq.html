Compact form of the prediction equation at the current time step

Combining all the prediction equations at $k$ into a single equation: $$ \begin{bmatrix} z_{k+1|k} \\ z_{k+2|k} \\ z_{k+3|k} \\ \vdots \\ z_{k+v|k} \\ z_{k+v+1|k} \\ \vdots \\ z_{k+p|k} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} CA \\ CA^2 \\ CA^3 \\ \vdots \\ CA^c \\ CA^{c+1} \\ \vdots \\ CA^p \end{bmatrix} x_k + \begin{bmatrix} CB & 0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ CAB & CB & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ CA^2B & CAB & CB & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \cdots & \cdots & \vdots \\ CA^{c-1}B & CA^{c-2}B & CA^{c-3}B & \cdots & CAB & CB \\ CA^cB & CA^{c-1}B & CA^{c-2}B & \cdots & CA^2B & C\bar{A}_{c+1|c}B \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \cdots & \cdots & \vdots \\ CA^pB & CA^{p-1}B & CA^{p-2}B & \cdots & CA^{p-c+1}B & C\bar{A}_{p|c}B \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{k|k} \\ u_{k+1|k} \\ u_{k+2|k} \\ \vdots \\ u_{k+c-2|k} \\ u_{k+c-1|k} \end{bmatrix} $$ This is written compactly as: $$ z = Ox_k + Mu $$
