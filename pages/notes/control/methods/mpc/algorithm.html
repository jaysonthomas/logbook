Algorithm

Steps

  • At time instant $k$, on the basis of the known state vector $x_k$ and the system matrices $A,B,C$, form the lifted matrices and compute the solution $\hat{u}$.

  • Extract the first entry of the solution $\hat{u}_{k|k}$
    • and apply it to the system.

  • Wait for the system to respond and obtain the state measurement $x_{k+1}$. Repeat step 1.
