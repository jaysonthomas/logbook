EMA: Cut-off frequency

Overview

$$\begin{align*} f_c &= \frac{\alpha}{2\pi t_s (1-\alpha)} \\ \frac{1}{2t_{ss}} &= \frac{\alpha}{2\pi t_s (1-\alpha)} \\ 1 - \alpha &= \frac{t_{ss}}{\pi t_s} \,\alpha \\ 1 &= \frac{t_{ss}}{\pi t_s} \,\alpha + \alpha \\ 1 &= (\frac{1}{\pi \frac{t_s}{t_{ss}}} + 1) \,\alpha \\ 1 &= (\frac{1 + \pi \frac{t_s}{t_{ss}}}{\pi \frac{t_s}{t_{ss}}}) \,\alpha \\ \alpha &= \frac{\pi \frac{t_s}{t_{ss}}}{1 + \pi \frac{t_s}{t_{ss}}} \\ \alpha &= \frac{\pi}{\frac{t_{ss}}{t_s} + \pi} \end{align*}$$
