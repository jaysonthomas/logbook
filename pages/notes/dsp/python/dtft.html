Aim

The aim is to extract frequency information from a discrete time signal.

Mathematical representation

$$ X_k = \sum^{N-1}_{n=0} x_n e^{-i2\pi k\frac{n}{N}} $$
$$\begin{array}{ccl} \displaystyle\sum^{N-1}_{n=0} &:& \text{Integral replaced by sum over $N$ samples of the input signal} \\ e^{-i2\pi k\frac{n}{N}} &:& \text{Contains amplitude and phase info for frequency $k$.} \\ && \text{Divides each sample with the corresponding complex number of that frequency}\\ && \text{as in the case of the fourier transform}\\ \end{array}$$
