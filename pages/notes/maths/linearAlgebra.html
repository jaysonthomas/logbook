Affine combination

An affine combination of two numbers is a linear combination of those numbers plus a constant. It is an expression of the form: $$ ax + by + c $$ $a, b, c$ are constants and $x,y$ are the two numbers being combined. The affine combination of two numbers is always a number that lies on the line segment connecting those two numbers.

The affine combination of 2 and 3 with $a = 1/2$, $b = 1/2$, and $c = 1$ is: $2.5$.
