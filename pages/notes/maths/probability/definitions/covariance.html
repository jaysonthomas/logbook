Covariance

Use

A covariance matrix summarises the pairwise correlations between variables in a dataset. It quantifies the spread, uncertainty, or variability of a dataset.

Features

A covariance matrix is
  • A square matrix.
  • Symmetric when the covariance between variables $i$ and $j$ (2D example) is the same as that between variables $j$ and $i$.

Mathematical expression

Zero covariance
