Operations related to unit vectors

Derivatives

$$\begin{align*} \hat{d} &= -\hat{j}\,cos\theta + \hat{i}\,sin\theta \\ \hat{q} &= \hat{j}\,sin\theta + \hat{i}\,cos\theta \\\\ \dot{\hat{q}} &= \hat{j}\,\dot\theta cos\theta - \hat{i}\,\dot\theta sin\theta \\ &= -\dot\theta \, \hat{d} \\ \dot{\hat{d}} &= \hat{j}\,\dot\theta sin\theta + \hat{i}\,\dot\theta cos\theta \\ &= \dot\theta \, \hat{q} \\\\ \ddot{\hat{q}} &= -\ddot\theta \, \hat{d} - \dot\theta^2\,\hat{q} \\ \ddot{\hat{d}} &= \ddot\theta \, \hat{q} - \dot\theta^2 \, \hat{d} \end{align*}$$
