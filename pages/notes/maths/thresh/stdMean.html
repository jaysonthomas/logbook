Using standard deviation & mean

Overview

$$ B = \mu \cdot k_\mu + \sigma \cdot k_\sigma $$
$$\begin{array}{ccl} \mu &:& \text{Moving average or baseline or expected background level} \\ k_\mu &:& \text{Scaling factor or multiplier for the baseline} \\ \sigma &:& \text{The typical amount of fluctuation/jitter in the signal} \end{array}$$
The 2 multipliers just provide different knobs to calculate the final threshold.
