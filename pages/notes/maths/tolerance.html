Tolerance

Relative Tolerance

$$ \lvert 1-\frac{a}{b} \rvert < \text{Tolerance value} $$ where $a$ and $b$ are the 2 numbers to be compared. $b$ is the reference value and must not be 0.

Absolute Tolerance

$$ \lvert a-b \rvert < \text{Tolerance value} $$
