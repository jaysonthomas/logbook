Alignment

Miscellaneous

  • When no numbering is required, alignment can be done with \begin{align*} and \end{align*}

  • To have numbers on certain lines, use \begin{align} along with \nonumber at the end of the line before \\

  • Left alignment of equations
    • Ref. Use & to specify the points in lines that we want aligned with each other.

Aligning multiple parts


  $$\begin{alignat*}{3}
    \min_x \quad & f(x)  \qquad && f(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\
    s.t. \quad & C(x) = 0  \qquad && C(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^m
  \end{alignat*}$$

Output

$$\begin{alignat*}{3} \min_x \quad & f(x) \qquad && f(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\ s.t. \quad & C(x) = 0 \qquad && C(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^m \end{alignat*}$$
