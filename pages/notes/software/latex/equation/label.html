Labeling

Overview

  • Underbrace
    • $$u = \underbrace{a_b}_{\text{Test1}} - \underbrace{b_a}_{\text{Test2}}$$ \underbrace{The part of the equation to be labelled }_{\text{The label}}
    [Ref] To change the fontsize of the text, place \scriptsize for example, before \text.

  • Multiple lines (only 2) in underbrace
    • Ref. \underbrace{}_{\text{} \atop \text{}} works well.

  • Another way of doing multiple lines ($>2$)
    • Add scripsize to make the text font smaller: $$\underbrace{M}_{\scriptsize\substack{\text{t1}\\\text{t2}\\\text{t3}}}$$
