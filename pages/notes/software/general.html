Json

This shows the general format. I used it as: 

  [
    {"id":"44", "name":"Test"},
    {"id":"55", "name":"Test1"}
  ]
To convert this into a dataframe: 

  with open(inputFile, "r") as reader:
    df = pd.DataFrame(json.loads(reader))
Use json.load() to read a json file.

Drawio

  • To type mathematical equations, enable Extras - Mathematical typesetting.
    • To type an equation use `` or $$ $$
  • Shortcuts
    • Ctrl-Shift-P to make the sidebar disappear
    • Ctri-Shift-u to ungroup
    • Ctrl-Shift-G to hide the grid
    • Ctrl-Shift-K to hide the left bar

    • Zoom - 175%, 10 point grid, 18px font size.
