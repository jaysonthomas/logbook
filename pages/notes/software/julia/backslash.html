Backslash operator

Description

When presented with x = A \ b, Julia tries to solve the linear equation: $$ A x = b $$ Instead of manually calculating an inverse ($A^{-1}b$), which is computationally expensive and numerically unstable, \ analyses the structure of the matrix $A$ (e.g., is it diagonal? symmetric? upper triangular?) and chooses the fastest, most accurate solver—usually based on LU or QR or LDLT decomposition.
