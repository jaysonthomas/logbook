Dynamics

The aim is to simulate the dynamics of a simple pendulum.

The pendulum dynamics function returns $$ \dot{x} = \begin{bmatrix} \dot\theta \\ \ddot\theta \end{bmatrix} $$

Notation

Notation followed: Jacobian notation.

State

Initial state is a guess. It is a column vector. $$ x_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0.1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}_{n \times 1} $$
