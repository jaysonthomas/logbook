c) \cdot (f_b - f_a) \biggr] \cdot f_a} {(f_c-f_a) \cdot (f_a-f_b) \cdot (f_c-f_b)} \\\\ \end{align*}$$ Splitting the terms: $$\begin{align*} \frac{p}{q} &= \frac{f_b \biggl[(a-b) \cdot f_c \cdot (f_c -
