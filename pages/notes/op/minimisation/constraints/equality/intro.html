Equality constraints

Setup

$$\begin{alignat*}{3} \min_x \quad & f(x) \qquad && f(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\ s.t. \quad & c(x) = 0 \qquad && c(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^m \end{alignat*}$$ The output of $c$ can be thought of as $m$ scalar constraints.

Usecase

Equalities are usually dynamics constraints. They can also be a goal constraint, for e.g. the aim could be to get to a particular state at the end of a trajectory or horizon etc.
