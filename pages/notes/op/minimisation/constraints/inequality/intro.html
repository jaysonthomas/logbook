Introduction to inequality constraints

Setup

$$\begin{alignat*}{3} \min_x \quad & f(x) \qquad && f(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\ s.t. \quad & c(x) \geq 0 \qquad && c(x) &&: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^m \end{alignat*}$$ The output of $c$ can be thought of as $m$ scalar constraints. The constraints are positive rather than positive definite.

Usecase

Inequalities show up in 2 areas:
  • On controls to reflect actuator (torque, thrust) limits.
  • On states to highlight safety constraints like keep-outs.
