Contents

DP equations can be equivalently written in terms of action-value or Q function: $$ Q_k(x, u) = l(x, u) + V_{k+1}(f(x, u)) $$ In model free RL, combining the cost-to-go, the one step cost and the dynamics into 1 function means that RL would only have to learn the 1 function (and implicitly learn the dynamics). In model based RL, the functions are separated out.
