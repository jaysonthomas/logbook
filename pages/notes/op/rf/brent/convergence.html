Brent's method: Convergence check

Setup

$$\begin{align*} m &= \frac{a-b}{2} \\ tol &= 2\cdot \epsilon \cdot max(|b|, 1) \end{align*}$$
$$\begin{array}{ccl} a-b &:& \text{Full step} \\ m &:& \text{Half step} \\ \epsilon &:& 2^{-23} =1.192 \times 10^{-7} \text{considering single (32 bit floating point) precision format} \end{array}$$

Exit check

$$ |m| \leq tol \quad || \quad f(b) == 0 $$
