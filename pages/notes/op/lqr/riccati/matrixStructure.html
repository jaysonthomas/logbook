Matrix structure

Derivation

The KKT system for LQR is very sparse (lots of 0s) and has lots of structure.

If there is no coupling, the matrix will be diagonal. Here everything (costs etc) is per time step. The only coupling across time steps happens in the dynamics constraints. In particular time steps $k$ and $k+1$ are coupled. But $k$ is not coupled to $k+2$. This means there is a single off diagonal element coupling between $k$ and $k+1$ and then after that nothing (just 0s).

$$\begin{bmatrix} R & & & & & & | & B^T & & \\ & Q & & & & & | & -I & A^T & \\ & & R & & & & | & & B^T & \\ & & & Q & & & | & & -I & A^T \\ & & & & R & & | & & & B^T \\ & & & & & Q_4 & | & & & -I \\ \hline B & -I & & & & & | & & & \\ & A & B & -I & & & | & & \mathbf{0} & \\ & & & A & B & -I & | & & & \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ x_2 \\ u_2 \\ x_3 \\ u_3 \\ x_4 \\ \hline \lambda_2 \\ \lambda_3 \\ \lambda_4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \\ \mathbf{0} \\ \\ \hline -Ax_1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}$$ Zooming into the bottom row of $H$: $$\begin{aligned} Q_4 x_4 - \lambda_4 &= 0 \\ \lambda_4 &= Q_4 x_4 \end{aligned}$$ Zooming into one row above and substituting $\lambda_4$: $$\begin{aligned} R u_3 + B^T \lambda_4 &= 0 \\ R u_3 + B^T Q_4 x_4 &= 0 \end{aligned}$$ Plugging in the dynamics for $x_4$, the above equation becomes: $$ R u_3 + B^T Q_4 (A x_3 + B u_3) = 0 $$ Rearranging $$\begin{equation} u_3 = -\underbrace{(R + B^T Q_4 B)^{-1} B^T Q_4 A}_{K_3} x_3 \label{eq:1} \end{equation}$$ Zooming into one row above again, substituting $\lambda_4$ and plugging in the dynamics for $x_4$: $$\begin{aligned} Q_3 x_3 - \lambda_3 + A^T \lambda_4 &= 0 \\ Q_3 x_3 - \lambda_3 + A^T Q_4 x_4 &= 0 \\ Q_3 x_3 - \lambda_3 + A^T Q_4 (A x_3 + B u_3) &= 0 \\ \end{aligned}$$ Rearranging and substituting $K_3$ to get an equation in just $x$: $$ \lambda_3 = \underbrace{(Q_3 + A^T Q_4 (A - B K_3))}_{P_3} x_3 $$
