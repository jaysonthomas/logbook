Riccati equation

Steps

There is a recursion for $K$ and $P$. Start the recursion at time step $N$: $$ P_N = Q_N $$ Then back-substitute to solve for $P$ and $K$. This runs from $N$ to the first time step. $$\begin{aligned} K_k &= (R + B^T P_{k+1} B)^{-1} B^T P_{k+1} A \\ P_k &= Q + A^T P_{k+1} (A - B K_k) \end{aligned}$$ This is called the Riccati equation/recursion.

There are 4 different versions of the Ricatti equation: discrete, continuous time and finite/infinite horizon.

Overview

First, solve the QP by doing a backward Riccati pass to get $K$s and $P$s. Then, do a forward pass by rolling out the dynamics to get $x_{1:N}$ and $u_{1:N-1}$.
