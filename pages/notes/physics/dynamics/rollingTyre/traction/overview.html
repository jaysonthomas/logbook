Overview

Traction force is the reaction force that actually propels a vehicle (or any object) forward. It is also called longitudinal friction force and acts in the direction of motion.
$$\begin{array}{ccl} F_m &:& \text{Applied motive force} \\ F &:& \text{Reaction traction force} \\ \tau &:& \text{Resistive torque} \\ \tau_m &:& \text{Motor torque} \\ \end{array}$$
