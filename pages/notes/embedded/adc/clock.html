Setting up the ADC clock

Overview

Considering an STM32F4 microcontroller with a HCLK of 180MHz. $$ \text{ADCCLK} = \frac{\text{PCLK2}}{\text{ADC_Prescaler}} $$ PCLK2 is the clock frequency of APB2 which is usually 90MHz.

To achieve a 22.5MHz ADC clock frequency, the prescaler would have to be $4$.
