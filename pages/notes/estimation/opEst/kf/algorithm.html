Algorithm

Steps

The first pass involves bootstrapping the recursion.
  • Start with the gaussian prior
    • $$ \hat x_{0|0} \; \Sigma_{0|0} \; W \; V $$

  • Prediction step
    • Pass the mean and the covariance through the linear dynamics. $$\begin{align*} \hat{x}_{k+1|k} &= A \hat{x}_{k|k} + B u_k \\ \Sigma_{k+1|k} &= A \Sigma_{k|k} A^T + W \end{align*}$$

  • Compute Innovation terms
    • $$\begin{align*} z_{k+1} &= y_{k+1} - C\hat{x}_{k+1|k} \\ S_{k+1} &= C \Sigma_{k+1|k} C^T + V \end{align*}$$

  • Compute Kalman Gain
    • $$ L_{k+1} = \Sigma_{k+1|k} C^\top S_{k+1}^{-1} $$

  • Measurement update
    • $$\begin{align*} \hat{x}_{k+1|k+1} &= \hat{x}_{k+1|k} + L_{k+1} z_{k+1} \\ \Sigma_{k+1|k+1} &= (I - L_{k+1}C) \Sigma_{k+1|k} (I - L_{k+1}C)^\top + L_{k+1} V L_{k+1}^\top \end{align*}$$

  • Go to prediction step.
