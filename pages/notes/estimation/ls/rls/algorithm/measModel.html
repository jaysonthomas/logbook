Measurement model

Overview

A measurement model encapsulated in the Jacobian matrix $H$:
  • Projects a state into its measurement space or
  • Maps a state to a measurement
$H$ contains partial derivatives of the measurements with respect to the unknown parameter/state(s) $x$. The measurement could be a linear or non-linear function of the true state. $H$ is easy to compute in the linear case but requires more mathematical effort in the case of non-linear estimation.
