Estimator initialisation

Initialise the estimate and the covariance matrix. The initial estimate could come from the first measurement $y_0$ and the covariance could come from technical specifications. $$\begin{align*} \hat{x}_0 &= \mathbb{E}[x] \\ P_0 &= \mathbb{E}[(x-\hat{x}_0)(x-\hat{x}_0)^T] \end{align*}$$
