Measurement equation description

Overview

As part of the measurement equation, models of the measurement and noise need to be defined. In linear least squares, the measurement equation is assumed to be linear. $$ y_k = H_kx + v_k $$
$$\begin{array}{cclc} y &:& \text{Observations or measurements} & n \times 1 \\ H &:& \text{Jacobian or measurement model} & n \times m \\ x &:& \text{State vector} & m \times 1 \\ v &:& \text{Noise model} & n \times 1 \\ k &:& \text{Time step} \end{array}$$
