Aim

The aim is to find the state that is most consistent with the measurement. Intuitively, the approach looks at the measurement and asks what state/parameter value is most likely to yield the measurement.

Mathematically, the goal is to select an estimate $\hat{x}$ to be the value of $x$ that maximises the probability of the actual measurements $y$ i.e. choose $\hat{x}$ to maximise $p(y| x = \hat{x})$
