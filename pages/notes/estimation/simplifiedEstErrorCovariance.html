RLS: An even more simplified form of the estimation error covariance

Using the gain matrix derived here: $$\begin{align*} K_k &= P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1} \\ \text{where,} \qquad L_k &= H_kP_{k-1}H_k^T + R_k \end{align*}$$ $L_k$ and its inverse are symmetric. Therefore: $$\begin{align*} L_k &= L_k^T \\ (L_k^{-1})^T &= L_k^{-1} \end{align*}$$ Substituting the expressions for $K_k$ and $L_k$ into the expanded version of the estimation error covariance propagation equation: $$\begin{align*} P_k &= P_{k-1} - K_kH_kP_{k-1} - P_{k-1}H_k^TK_k^T + K_kH_kP_{k-1}H_k^TK_k^T + K_kR_kK^T_k \\ &= P_{k-1} - P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1}H_kP_{k-1} - P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1}H_kP_{k-1} + K_k(\underbrace{H_kP_{k-1}H_k^T + R_k}_{L_k})K^T_k \\ &= P_{k-1} - 2P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1}H_kP_{k-1} + P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1}H_kP_{k-1}\\ &= P_{k-1} - \underbrace{P_{k-1}H_k^TL_k^{-1}}_{K_k}H_kP_{k-1}\\ &= P_{k-1} - K_kH_kP_{k-1}\\ &= (I - K_kH_k)P_{k-1}\\ \end{align*}$$
