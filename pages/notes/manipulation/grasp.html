Benefits of soft robots

  • Inherently safe

  • Robust to geometric uncertainty
    • Inherently less dependent on perception or geometric models which have uncertainty and therefore are more robust.

    An accurate point cloud registration of the object being manipulated along with its pose isn't required. All that is required is to place or get close to the oject at roughly the right location.
