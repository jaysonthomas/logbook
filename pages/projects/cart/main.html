Setup

Consider that the mass is 1kg and the control period is 1Hz.

Math

Resolving the forces in the x-direction, the only DOF of the system: $$\begin{align*} ma &= F - F_f \\ a &= \frac{F - F_f}{m} \end{align*}$$ Using forward-euler integration to generate a discrete time simulation of the system: $$\begin{align*} a_k &= \frac{F_k - F_{f_k}}{m} \\ v_k &= v_{k-1} + a_k \cdot \Delta{t} \\ p_k &= p_{k-1} + v_k \cdot \Delta{t} \end{align*}$$
