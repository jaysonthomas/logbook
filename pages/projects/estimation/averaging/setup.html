Averaging: Setup

Overview

The aim is to control a coaxial drone using measurements obtained by an altitude sensor.
$$\begin{array}{cclc} x_t &:& \text{True state of the drone at time t} \\ z_t &:& \text{Measurement (of altitude) made at time t} \\ \hat{x}_t &:& \text{Estimate of the true state at time t} \end{array}$$
The sensor used by the quadrotor estimates the altitude with a weighted average defined as: $$ \hat{x}_t = \alpha \hat{x}_{t-1} + (1-\alpha)z_t $$ At $t=0$, the drone's position and velocity are $z = \dot{z} = 0$. At this exact time, the drone's been asked to change its position by 1 meter in the negative direction. It's desired altitude will be $z=-1$.
