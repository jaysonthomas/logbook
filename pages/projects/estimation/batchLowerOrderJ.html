Measurement equation

$$\begin{align*} y_{k \times 1} &= H_{k \times m} \; x_{m \times 1} + v_{k \times 1} \\ \begin{bmatrix} y_1 \\ \vdots \\ y_k \end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix} I_1 & 1 \\ \vdots & \vdots \\ I_k & 1 \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}R_1 \\ R_0 \end{bmatrix} \\ y &= IR_1 + R_0 + v \end{align*}$$
$$\begin{array}{ccl} m &:& \text{Number of input variables (here, $m=2$)} \\ k &:& \text{Number of measurements taken} \end{array}$$
