[Numpy implementation]

For simulation purposes, random gaussian noise is added to the true state, to get the measured state. $$ \hat{x}_t = x_t + \epsilon_t $$
$$\begin{array}{ccl} x_t &:& \text{True state of the drone at time t} \\ \hat{x}_t &:& \text{Measurement (or measured state, mainly the altitude, here) at time t} \\ \epsilon_t &:& \text{Random variable drawn from a Gaussian (Normal) distribution }\mathcal{N}\\ && \text{with a mean of 0 and a variance of } \sigma^2 \end{array}$$
$$ \epsilon_t \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma^2) $$
