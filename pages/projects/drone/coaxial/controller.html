Averaging: Controller

Overview

Error terms: $$\begin{align*} e &= x_d - \hat{x} \\ \dot{e} &= \dot{x}_d - \dot{\hat{x}} \\ e_i &= e_i + (e \cdot \Delta{t}) \end{align*}$$ PID terms: $$\begin{align*} P &= k_p \cdot e \\ D &= k_d \cdot \dot{e} \\ I &= k_i \cdot e_i \end{align*}$$ New torque demand: $$ u = P + I + D + \ddot{x} $$
