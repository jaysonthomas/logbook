Reference frame

Definition

A reference frame has a point at its centre. Its orientation can be represented in many ways but it is visually represented with the xyz axes. XYZ => RGB. The right-hand rule (vehicle coordinate system) is used.

The aim is to get different reference frames to match; because once they do, the algebra becomes trivial. E.g., once reference frames match, positions can be added.
